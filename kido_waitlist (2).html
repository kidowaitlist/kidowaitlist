The Problem

Getting kids to school shouldn't be this stressful.

Every morning across San Ramon, the same frustrating situation plays out — and families have been putting up with it for way too long.

🚗

Traffic every single morning

San Ramon has a high concentration of schools within a small area — and when they all start and end at similar times, the streets around them become completely gridlocked. The same bottleneck, every single morning and every single afternoon.

⏰

No time, no flexibility

Between getting kids ready and rushing to make it on time, there's barely a moment to breathe. Missing the window even slightly means a stressful scramble that throws off the entire day.

😓

No good options exist

Traditional school buses don't reach every neighborhood and aren't always reliable. The only alternative most families have is handling it themselves — every single day.

⚠️

Rush hour creates real danger

Crowded school zones, impatient drivers, and distracted parents in a hurry are a dangerous combination. The morning school rush causes more accidents than most people realize.

How It Works

Simple, safe, and stress-free.

We handle everything from pickup to drop-off so you can focus on your day.

1📋

Sign up your child

Create a profile with your child's school, address, and schedule. Quick, simple, and we handle everything from there.

2🗺️

We build your route

Your child is assigned to a shared route headed to their school. Routes are planned to be efficient and consistent every single day.

3🚐

Your driver arrives on time

A vetted, background-checked driver picks your child up from your front door at a set time — morning, afternoon, or both.

4📍

Track every ride live

Follow your child's exact location in real time from the moment they're picked up to when they arrive safely at school.

5🔔

Stay informed automatically

Get notified at every key moment — pickup, school arrival, and when your child is on their way back home. You're always in the loop.

6☕

Enjoy your morning

Get to where you need to be on time, every day. No traffic, no rushing, no stress. Just a calm, normal morning — finally.
